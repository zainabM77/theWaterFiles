🏛️ Contact Your Rep

Copy this letter, find your MP, hit send, 3 minutes
Dear [MP's name], I am a nursing student and constituent writing about Canada's unfinished promise of safe drinking water for First Nations. As of this year, roughly 37 First Nations communities remain under long-term drinking water advisories, including Neskantaga First Nation, which has boiled its water for over 30 years. The Auditor General has rated Canada's progress "unsatisfactory." Water is a human right, recognized by UN Resolution 64/292. I am asking you to: 1. Support the reintroduction and passage of First Nations clean-water legislation (the successor to Bill C-61), with guaranteed, sustainable funding; 2. Close the annual operations-and-maintenance funding gap for on-reserve water systems identified by the Parliamentary Budget Officer; 3. Support regulation requiring industrial and data-centre water users to report and reduce consumption in water-stressed regions. As a future nurse, I see the downstream cost of unsafe water in human health. I would welcome your response outlining your position. Respectfully, [Your name] [Your riding]
